## Entries

### DeepLabCut

- **Type**: Library
- **Supplier**: Mathis Labs (EPFL / Harvard) ([deeplabcut.github.io](https://deeplabcut.github.io/DeepLabCut/))
- **Availability**: GA (3.0 with PyTorch engine)
- **Pricing**: Free / OSS (LGPL v3.0); SuperAnimal pretrained models restricted to non-commercial research
- **Capabilities**: Read/Write — markerless 2D/3D pose estimation for arbitrary species
- **Primary use cases**: Behavioral neuroscience, kinematics tracking, multi-animal social behavior assays
- **Benchmarks**: Mathis et al., _Nature Neuroscience_ (2018); Lauer et al., _Nature Methods_ (2022) — multi-animal pose estimation evaluation
- **Installation**: `pip install 'deeplabcut[gui]'` (Python 3.10+); PyTorch (and optional CUDA) required
- **Integration notes**: Companion repos include DLC-Live (real-time SDK), DLC2NWB (Neurodata Without Borders export), napari-deeplabcut
- **Sources**: [GitHub](https://github.com/DeepLabCut/DeepLabCut), [Documentation](https://deeplabcut.github.io/DeepLabCut/), [DeepLabCut-Live](https://github.com/DeepLabCut/DeepLabCut-live)
